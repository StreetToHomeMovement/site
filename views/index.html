We are a grassroots movement supporting those in our community that have been homeless the longest in obtaining permanent housing, recovery, and community re-integration.

We know 109 South Brevard residents by name that have been living on our streets for over a year

Our Street Outreach Team brings housing specialists and law enforcement together to conduct planned morning, day, and night shifts to engage those living on the street and move the supportive housing process forward.

Someone's father, mother, brother, sister, daughter, son

Each person experiencing homelessness is someone: someone's father, mother, brother, sister, daughter, son. Someone capable of change. Our team of support professionals focuses on evoking each person's own ability for change. And change starts with a home.
